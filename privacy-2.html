Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

1. Introduction

Flōw ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social media management platform at thesocialflow.xyz.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and password (stored as an encrypted hash via Supabase Auth).

Content You Create: Posts, captions, drafts, scheduled content, content ideas, pitch decks, and contacts you enter into the app.

Platform Connections: When you connect social media accounts, we store access tokens and basic profile information (username, platform ID) required to authenticate with those platforms. We never store your passwords for third-party platforms.

Usage Data: We may collect anonymized information about how you interact with the app to improve the product.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the Flōw platform
  • Authenticate you and secure your account
  • Save your content, schedules, and settings across sessions
  • Connect to social media APIs on your behalf
  • Generate AI-powered content using the Anthropic Claude API (your prompts are sent to Anthropic — see Section 5)
  • Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • Improve and develop our services

4. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure, which uses PostgreSQL databases with row-level security — meaning your data is cryptographically scoped to your account only. No other user can access your data.

We implement industry-standard security practices including encrypted connections (TLS), hashed passwords, and secure token storage.

5. Third-Party Services

Flōw integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Anthropic (Claude API): Your AI prompts are sent to Anthropic for processing. See Anthropic's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and storage. See Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: Hosting and serverless functions. See Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • Google / YouTube: When connected, YouTube API Services are used. Flōw's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
  • Other Social Platforms: Instagram, Facebook, Twitter/X, LinkedIn, TikTok, and Snapchat APIs may be used when you connect those accounts. Each platform's own privacy policy governs their data handling.

6. Google User Data

When you connect your YouTube or Google account, Flōw accesses only the data necessary to display your channel analytics and manage your YouTube content scheduling. Specifically, we request access to your YouTube channel statistics and upload capabilities.

We do not sell, share, or use your Google user data for advertising purposes. Your Google data is used solely to provide the features you explicitly request within Flōw. You can revoke access at any time by disconnecting your Google account in the Connections settings or via your Google Account permissions page.

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We do not share your data except with service providers who help operate the platform, if required by law, or to protect our rights.

8. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all data we hold about you
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Portability: Export your content and data via Settings → Export
  • Disconnect: Revoke social media platform connections at any time in Settings → Connected Accounts

9. Cookies

Flōw uses only essential cookies necessary for authentication (session tokens). We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking cookies.

10. Children's Privacy

Flōw is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by displaying a prominent notice in the app.

12. Contact

For privacy-related questions or to exercise your rights, contact us at: privacy@thesocialflow.xyz
